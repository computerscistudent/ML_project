Welcome to my ML project!!

This project is a Student Exam Performance Predictor. It takes in basic details about a student—like their gender, family background, lunch type, test preparation, and their reading and writing scores—and then uses a machine learning model to predict how well the student is likely to perform in math. In simple words, you give it some student information, and it will estimate their expected math score. This can help teachers, parents, and students understand performance patterns and identify areas where extra support might be needed.
